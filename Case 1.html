Order Delivery Optimization

Optimizing delivery routes and schedules is critical for e-commerce businesses. Algorithms like Dijkstra's, Bellman-Ford, and Floyd-Warshall can help find the shortest paths and optimal routes for deliveries, improving efficiency and reducing costs.

Data Structures:

  • Arrays/Vectors: Used to store locations, drivers, and deliveries.
  • Priority Queue: Helps in selecting the smallest distance or edge (used in Dijkstra's and Prim's algorithms).

Graph Algorithms:

  • Dijkstra's Algorithm:
    • Finds the shortest path between two cities.
    • It repeatedly selects the closest city and updates distances to other cities.
  • Travelling Salesman Problem:
    • Traveling Salesman Problem (TSP) aims to find the shortest possible route that visits each city once and returns to the starting point. It works by evaluating all possible routes (using brute force or optimization algorithms) to determine the minimal travel distance.
